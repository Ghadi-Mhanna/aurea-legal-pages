Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Aurea?
Simply download the app and start adding your first task, event, or expense. Aurea is designed to be intuitive â€“ no account setup or complex configuration required.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely! All your data is stored locally on your device. Aurea doesn't send your personal information to external servers or share it with third parties. You can also enable app lock with biometrics for additional security.
Can I sync my data across multiple devices?
Aurea stores data locally for maximum privacy. You can export your data and manually transfer it, or use your device's backup features to restore data when setting up the app on a new device.
How do notifications work?
Aurea can send local notifications for reminders and daily motivation. All notifications are generated on your device. You can customize notification preferences in the app settings or disable them entirely in your device settings.
What if I accidentally delete my data?
We recommend regularly backing up your device using iCloud or iTunes. If you've enabled device backups, your Aurea data will be included and can be restored when you restore your device.
Does Aurea work offline?
Yes! Aurea is designed to work completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection since everything is stored and processed locally on your device.

Troubleshooting

App Performance Issues

  • Restart the app by closing it completely and reopening
  • Ensure you have the latest version installed from the App Store
  • Restart your device if the app continues to be unresponsive
  • Check that you have sufficient storage space on your device

Notification Problems

  • Check notification permissions in iOS Settings > Aurea > Notifications
  • Verify notification settings within the Aurea app
  • Ensure Do Not Disturb mode isn't blocking notifications

App Lock Issues

  • Verify biometric authentication is enabled in your device settings
  • Try disabling and re-enabling app lock in Aurea settings
  • Ensure your device supports the biometric method you're trying to use
