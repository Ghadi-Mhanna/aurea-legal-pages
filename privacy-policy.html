Aurea — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 07 Oct 2025

Aurea is a personal productivity app for calendar planning, tasks, goals, and expense tracking. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes and how it is handled. This app is developed and operated by an individual developer (no company involved).

Key point: Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly share it.

Information We Process

Aurea stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • Calendar events, tasks, reminders, and goals
  • Expense entries and categories
  • App settings (theme, notification preferences, security lock)

No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties by default. The app does not implement analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote databases.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications for reminders and daily motivation. Notification content is generated on-device. You can change notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

Biometrics and App Lock

When you enable the optional app lock, Aurea uses the operating system’s biometrics (e.g., Face ID, Touch ID) or PIN interface to authenticate you. Biometric templates are never accessed by Aurea; verification is handled by the OS. Any lock PIN you set within the app is stored locally only.

Financial Data

Expense and income information you enter is stored locally on your device. Aurea does not connect to bank accounts, payment providers, or financial institutions, and does not perform financial profiling.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Aurea does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. If you choose to export or share data using your device’s native share options, that action is initiated and controlled by you.

Children’s Privacy

Aurea is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information in the app, you can delete it directly within the app or by removing the app from the device.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally and remains until you delete it. You can clear data from within the app or by uninstalling the app, which removes all app data stored on the device.

Security Measures

Aurea relies on the security of your device and the operating system’s sandboxing. Optional app lock further restricts access. For best protection, keep your device updated and secured with a passcode and biometric lock.

International Transfers

No international data transfers occur because your data does not leave your device unless you choose to share or back it up using services you control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect app changes or legal requirements. Material changes will update the “Effective date” above.
